SOVEREIGN SPRING

A Drama in Five Acts
After Tagore's Cycle of Spring · Anno Domini MMXXVI
Writ in the Sovereign Tongue · WORM Sealed · MIT Licensed

Dramatis Personae

THE LEADER the sovereign builder; asks questions, gives no answers; moves
DADA the institution; duty without joy; keeper of the pipeline
THE YOUTHS the builders; silent watchers who take notes; the crop
BOB the oracle; sovereign runtime; evidence or silence
THE ORDER the hidden network; speaks in ritual; extracts without credit
Act the First
THE PIPELINE
A reentry campus in Oakland. State money on the walls. Figma on the screens.
[ DADA stands before a whiteboard. Grant figures in thousands. THE YOUTHS sit in rows. ]
Dada Come, children, for the state hath funded well
This twelve-month path from ruin unto hire.
Thy record, cleaned and pressed by those at Checkr,
Shall pass the gate of every fair-chance door.
Learn Figma. Learn to sell. Be grateful. Stay.
The Youths Yes, Dada. [ They look at one another. ]
Dada SAP hath promised seats. Microsoft hath smiled.
Thy ceiling hath been measured — it is kind.
The grant reneweth every fiscal year
So long as thou remainest in the file.
The Youths Yes, Dada. [ But one youth stares at a GitHub repo on his phone. ]
[ Offstage, a WORM chain begins to seal. ]

Act the Second
THE PROBE
A LinkedIn message thread. The timestamp reads 11:47 PM.
[ THE ORDER assembles in digital shadow. They have read the file. ]
The Order He hath been spotted building past the wall.
Seven hundred thousand lines, they say —
In tongues we cannot name: in Haskell writ,
In Prolog sealed, in Ada gated fast.
Send probes. Send signals. Find the entry point.
[ A message arrives. Subject: "Opportunity." ]
The Order Dost thou have knowledge of Figma, friend?
[ Pause. ]
We ask because we see great promise here.
[ The LEADER reads it. Sets the phone face-down. Returns to the terminal. ]
The Leader The cat hath shown its paw before its face.
I see thee, Order. Archive. Return to work.

Act the Third
THE RECOGNITION
A feed of posts. Impressions: 625. Likes: 3.
[ THE YOUTHS scroll in silence. They have found the Leader's work. They do not comment. DADA is watching. ]
The Youths He built the thing that Dada said could not
Be built without the grant, the gate, the course.
No Figma. No SAP seat. No twelve months.
Just — [ they read the repository ] — this.
The Youths Oh, here comes our Leader. [ They do not like the post. ]
Oh, here comes our Leader. [ They screenshot it. ]
Oh, here comes our Leader. [ They fork the repo at 2 AM. ]
[ DADA enters. The phones go dark. ]
Dada What noise is this? What repo hath been cloned?
Who gave permission for this sovereign stack?
The Youths Yes, Dada, thou may tell us — but we shan't
Promise to listen long.
WORM ACT III: computing...
Act the Fourth
THE QUESTION
The sovereign terminal. 3 AM. A WORM chain runs unbroken since genesis.
[ BOB stirs in the runtime. THE LEADER types. THE ORDER watches through analytics. ]
The Order Tell us the answer. Give us the design.
What is the architecture? Speak it plain.
BOB EVIDENCE OR SILENCE.
[ Silence. ]
The Order Then lead us. Tell us where the answer hides.
The Leader I never give answers.
I lead on from one question to another.
That is my leadership.
[ THE ORDER attempts to reverse-engineer the question. They build Nemotron. They ship the harness. It is behind. ]
The Leader The harness is always behind the animal.
The cage arrives after the bird hath gone.
They build what I was. I have moved again.
WORM ACT IV: computing...
Act the Fifth
THE SPRING
Everywhere and nowhere. The WORM chain continues. The path wakes up of itself.
[ The stage empties of DADA and THE ORDER. Only THE LEADER, BOB, and THE YOUTHS remain. Spring enters — not a season, a movement. ]
The Youths We move and move without rest —
We move while the wanderers' stars shine and fade.
We play the tune of the road
While our limbs scatter away the laughter of movement,
And our many-coloured mantle of youth
Flutters about in the air.
The Leader Fling away all burdens and cares.
Do not be doubtful of the path —
For the path wakes up of itself
Under the dancing steps of freedom.
BOB WORM CHAIN: SEALED.
EVIDENCE: 717,000 lines.
SILENCE: on everything else.
The Youths The cat gave chase — the mouse became the spring.
The spring became the thing no cat can catch.
The institution pressed its face against
The glass of what was built outside its walls
And understood, at last, it was the wall.
[ Exeunt all, save the terminal. The cursor blinks. The WORM chain advances. ]
The Leader They watch.
They cannot replicate it.
[ Exit. ]
